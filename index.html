Temperature Converter

Please input the temperature in Celsius (°C) or Fahrenheit (°F), and press the convert button to get the conversion results.

Calculation formula will appear here.

Conversion Formulas

From Celsius to Fahrenheit: S(°F) = (S(°C) × 9/5) + 32

From Fahrenheit to Celsius: S(°C) = (S(°F) - 32) × 5/9
